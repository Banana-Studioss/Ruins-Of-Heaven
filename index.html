Ruins Of Heaven

Ben and Mercedes enter a Holy Temple to claim the Gem of Innocence and fix their problems. Twenty-five rooms stand between them and it — puzzles that need both players, enemies that need both powers, and one very persistent skeleton. The demo gets you through Room 13. What happens after that, you'll have to earn.

Two Broken People

Nobody enters a temple like this by accident.

Ben and Mercedes aren't heroes. They're two people who need something fixed, and the Gem of Innocence is the only thing either of them has heard might do it. What they're each trying to fix is theirs to carry — the temple doesn't care, and neither does what's waiting at the end of it.

Two Players

Neither of you can finish this alone. That's the point.

Ben
Fire in the chest

Ben burns through what's in front of him — obstacles, enemies, patience. Grace saw that in him and gave him something to match it. What he can't burn through, he has to trust Mercedes to carry him over.

Mercedes
Flight, and the weight of it

Mercedes flies, which means she's the one who decides what gets left behind and what comes with her. Grace gave her that choice too — and made sure it costs her something every time she makes it.

The Temple

It starts calm. It does not stay calm.

You'll start outside, in grass and birdsong, before the temple asks anything hard of you. Somewhere past that, Grace is waiting — and so is everything she's willing to throw at you to see if you deserve what you came for. The demo takes you far enough in to mean it. What's past that, you'll have to buy your way into.

Editions

Start with the demo. Stay for the rest.

The demo is free and complete on its own terms — it's not a slice, it's a beginning. When you're ready to see the temple all the way through, Ruins Of Heaven and Ruins Of Heaven+ are both waiting, the latter open to anyone who wants to build on top of it.

Wiki

The basics

Enough to know what you're walking into. Nothing past that — we'd rather you find the rest yourself.

The Holy Temple

Where Ben and Mercedes are headed. Grounds first, then the temple proper — it gets harder the deeper in you go.

The Gem of Innocence

What they're both after. Said to fix whatever's broken in the person who claims it.

Grace

The Angel of Gods. She's the one who grants Ben and Mercedes their powers — and the one who decides if they've earned the gem.

Ben

Player One. Fire-tempered, and it shows in how he plays.

Mercedes

Player Two. Flies, and carries more than just Ben when she does.

Co-op, only

Ruins Of Heaven is built for two players. Neither Ben nor Mercedes can finish the temple without the other.
